Rice and Beans

description

Robust rice and beans! Great for a family gatherings or for dining alone. This dish is always a sensation!
(When you're on a budget)

ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Water

steps

For the rice

  1. Rince and strain the rice several times until the water is mostly clear.
  2. Strain the rice and place in your preffered cooking pot.
  3. Add two times the amount of water as there is for rice. ex. 1 cup of rice means 2 cups of water. Optional: Let the rice sit for an hour before cooking.
  4. Now we cook the rice. If you have a rice cooker then just put the rice and water into the rice cooker and set to cook. If not we will use the 5-5-5 rule. Turn the stove to high until the water comes to a boil. Once boiling turn it down to medium and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  5. Next, turn the heat dowm to very low and let sit for 5 minutes
  6. Finally, turn the stove top off and let sit for 5 minutes
  7. If the rice is too moist, stir it and then put the rice back on low heat without the lid for a few more minutes until it is to your liking.

For the beans

  1. Place beans in a saucepan and cover with 4 cups of water to 1 cup of beans.
  2. Bring to a boil for 2 minutes, then leave to stand for 1 hour.
  3. Now we cook the beans. Return the pan to the heat and simmer until beans are soft. 1-3 hours. Note: Check frequently to ensure not to overcook the beans.
  4. Finally, serve together on a dish, bowl, or cup and enjoy!
