Beans

Description

Delicious beans!
Great for a family gathering or for dining alone. This dish is always a sensation!(When you're on a budget)

Ingredients

  • Beans(dry)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place beans in a saucepan and cover with 4 cups of water to 1 cup of beans.
  2. Bring to a boil for 2 minutes, then leave to stand for 1 hour.
  3. Now we cook the beans. Return the pan to the heat and simmer until beans are soft. 1-3 hours
    Note: Check frequently to ensure not to overcook the beans.
